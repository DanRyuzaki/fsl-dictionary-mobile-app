
Welcome to FSL dictionary app!

Designed to improve communication for the deaf and hard of hearing. Founded by Aleah Jane Cabading, Azumi Yurie Javier, Cyryll Ballesteros, Zyra Lhoraine Cabagtong, and Danielle Adan.

Our goal is to empower users with FSL knowledge, promote understanding, and ensure equal access to information. 

We've created a comprehensive database of FSL signs to facilitate communication and enhance accessibility. 

Whether you're deaf, an interpreter, or an ally, our app is here to support you in learning FSL and fostering inclusivity. 

Thank you for choosing our app.
